# HCLS AI Factory Logo — Napkin AI Pro Prompt

> **Output:** Custom logo for website header (upper-left corner)
> **Format:** PNG with transparent background + dark background variant
> **Dimensions:** 280px wide × 60px tall (2x for retina: 560×120)
> **Style:** Modern, clean, professional healthcare/technology aesthetic

---

## Design Brief

Create a sophisticated logo wordmark for the **HCLS AI Factory** — a precision medicine accelerator platform that transforms patient DNA into drug candidates. The logo will appear in the upper-left corner of a dark navy website header.

---

## Text Elements

### Primary Text: "HCLS AI Factory"
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif with strong presence
- **Weight:** Bold/Heavy (700-800)
- **Size:** Primary hierarchy — roughly 18-20px equivalent when rendered
- **Tracking:** Slightly tight letter-spacing for cohesion
- **Color:** White (#FFFFFF) for legibility against dark backgrounds

### Secondary Text: "Precision Medicine Accelerator"
- **Typography:** Same typeface family, lighter weight
- **Weight:** Regular or Medium (400-500)
- **Size:** Approximately 40-50% of primary text height
- **Tracking:** Normal letter-spacing
- **Color:** Muted white (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.6) or #9CA3AF)
- **Position:** Directly below "HCLS AI Factory", left-aligned

---

## Visual Treatment Options

### Option A: Gradient Accent Mark
A small geometric icon to the left of the text:
- **Shape:** Abstract double helix or molecular node (3-4 connected circles)
- **Color:** Gradient from green (#76B900) → teal (#00B4D8) → purple (#8b5cf6)
- **Size:** Square, matching the height of both text lines combined
- **Style:** Minimal line art or filled geometric

### Option B: Gradient Text Initial
- **"HCLS"** rendered with the gradient (green → teal → purple)
- **"AI Factory"** in solid white
- No separate icon — the gradient letters serve as the visual anchor

### Option C: Subtle Icon Integration
- A small **DNA helix** or **circuit-node pattern** integrated into the negative space of a letter (like the "A" in "AI" or "Factory")
- Gradient applied only to this integrated element
- Rest of text in clean white

## Typography Recommendations

**Primary choices (modern, technical, healthcare-appropriate):**
1. **Inter** — Clean, highly legible, modern
2. **Space Grotesk** — Geometric, tech-forward
3. **Outfit** — Friendly but professional
4. **Manrope** — Balanced, versatile

**Avoid:**
- Serif fonts (too traditional for AI/tech)
- Overly rounded fonts (too playful)
- Condensed fonts (reduced legibility at small sizes)

---

## Mood & Style Keywords

- **Professional** — This is healthcare infrastructure, not a startup gimmick
- **Trustworthy** — Researchers and clinicians need to take this seriously
- **Modern** — AI-native, GPU-accelerated, cutting-edge technology
- **Clean** — Minimal ornamentation, clear hierarchy
- **Open** — Approachable, not corporate or locked-down
- **Precise** — Genomics and drug discovery demand accuracy

---

## What to Avoid

- Overly complex icons (must read at small sizes)
- Medical clichés (red crosses, stethoscopes, generic DNA clipart)
- Startup-y playfulness (bouncy letters, excessive gradients)
- Corporate stiffness (all-caps everything, generic blue)
- Busy backgrounds or textures

The logo must feel native to this environment — sophisticated, technical, and aligned with the visual language of GPU-accelerated healthcare AI.
